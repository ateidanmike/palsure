Fraud Protection & Security

At Palsure, we take fraud prevention seriously. The insurance industry has unfortunately been plagued by fraudulent schemes that take advantage of unsuspecting customers. We've built comprehensive safeguards to protect you throughout your insurance journey.

Our Security Measures

Verified Insurance Partners

We only work with licensed insurance companies registered with the Insurance Regulatory Authority (IRA) of Kenya. Every partner is thoroughly vetted and verified.

Real-time Verification

All policies and quotes are verified directly with insurance providers in real-time, ensuring authenticity and preventing fake policies.

Secure Data Protection

Your personal and financial information is encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology and stored securely in compliance with data protection laws.

Transparent Documentation

Every policy comes with complete, official documentation directly from the insurance provider. No altered or fake documents.

Direct Communication

We facilitate direct communication between you and the insurance provider, eliminating middlemen who might manipulate information.

Regulatory Compliance

Palsure operates under strict regulatory oversight and maintains compliance with all Kenyan insurance and financial services regulations.

How We Prevent Common Insurance Frauds

Fake Insurance Companies

The Problem: Fraudsters create fake insurance companies with professional-looking websites and documentation.

Our Solution: We maintain a database of all IRA-licensed insurers and only display verified companies. Each partner's license status is checked regularly.

Inflated Premiums

The Problem: Brokers sometimes inflate premiums and pocket the difference.

Our Solution: Our platform shows actual premiums directly from insurers with complete transparency in pricing breakdown.

Hidden Terms & Conditions

The Problem: Important policy exclusions or terms are hidden or misrepresented.

Our Solution: We provide complete, unmodified policy documents and highlight important terms in plain language.

Claims Payment Issues

The Problem: Fraudulent schemes often fail to pay legitimate claims.

Our Solution: We only partner with financially stable, reputable insurers with proven track records of claims payment.

Red Flags We Help You Avoid

  • Unusually low premiums that seem too good to be true
  • Pressure to pay immediately without proper documentation
  • Requests for payment to personal accounts rather than company accounts
  • Lack of proper company registration or insurance license
  • Promises of instant coverage without proper underwriting
  • Unwillingness to provide complete policy documentation
