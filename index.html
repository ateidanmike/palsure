Fraud Prevention & Security

Your security is our priority. Learn how we protect you from insurance fraud and ensure transparent transactions.

Insurance fraud is a serious problem in Kenya, costing consumers millions annually. At Palsure, we've implemented comprehensive security measures to ensure you're always dealing with legitimate insurers and genuine policies.

Common Insurance Frauds

  • Fake insurance companies and brokers
  • Policies that don't exist or aren't valid
  • Hidden charges and inflated premiums
  • Claims that are never processed
  • Unlicensed agents selling fake policies

Our Protection Measures

  • IRA-verified insurers only
  • Real-time policy verification
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Direct insurer partnerships
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden fees

How We Ensure Your Safety

IRA Verification

All our partner insurers are verified and regulated by the Insurance Regulatory Authority of Kenya.

Real-time Validation

Every policy is validated in real-time with the insurer's database to ensure authenticity.

Secure Transactions

24/7 Support
